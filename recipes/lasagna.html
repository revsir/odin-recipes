World's Best Lasagna

Description

Have you ever had something so delectable that it was a game changer for the rest of your life? This lasagna recipe is just that. It's the best lasagna recipe I've ever had and I'm proud to share it with you.

This recipe is super easy to make and there is so much for you to share with your loved ones. Just follow this simple recipe and you'll be on your way to a delicious meal. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbps white sugar
  • 4 tbps chopped parsley
  • 1 1/2 tsp dried basil leaves
  • 1 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1lb mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
